About

此處不留人，但所有人都可以留下來。

This place keeps no one, yet everyone is welcome to stay.

沒有攻略，沒有教條，沒有大道理。只是有些話，僅在微醺時才吐得出口。

No guides, no doctrine, no grand truths. Only a few words that can only be spat out when one is a bit tipsy.

所以人要關注酒精，也要關注酒後之言。

So one must pay attention to alcohol, and to the words that follow.

因此，英倫酒精關注組確實存在。

So this place exists.

就像世上任何一張吧檯一樣，獨酌係一種樂趣，圍埋又係另一種。

Like every bar counter in the world, drinking alone is one kind of pleasure; gathering round is another.

你好，我是言又勤。
