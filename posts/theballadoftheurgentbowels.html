The Ballad of the Urgent Bowels

2026 · 05 · 21  ·  散文

接駁巴士開出 忽聞腹中哀號 是血脈翻騰急流 是江河大浪激奔

The shuttle bus pulls away. Lo, a lament rises from the deep. Blood surges into torrent. Rivers rage and thunder.

理智失　冷汗掉 書已不能讀了 有話想說未敢說 可惡的交通紅燈

Reason falters. Cold sweat falls. The printed page is unreadable now. Words unsaid press against the teeth. Damn this red light. Damn it.

闔上眼　眉頭緊 咬實牙關死忍 狂轉人生走馬燈 深呼吸有助降溫

Eyes shut. Brow locked. Teeth clenched. Hold. Life's reel spins its final frames. Breathe. Breathe. Cool the flames.

達陣　即將達陣 跑上兩層向右奔 達陣　終於達陣 一蹲春風吹又生

Touchdown. Almost touchdown. Up two flights, turn right, run. Touchdown. At last, touchdown. One squat, and the grass grows green again.
